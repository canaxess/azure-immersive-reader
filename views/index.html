Azure Immersive Reader

This shows how the Immersive Reader tool could be used to assist a users literacy and comprehension. Websites may have sections of text at an advanced reading age, the tool could help a user to understand individual words, sections of text or the entire content.

Additionally, it could be used as technique to pass more significant WCAG 2.1 success criteria 3.1.3 Unusual Words AAA, 3.1.5 Reading Level AAA and 3.1.6 Pronunciation AAA.

How to use

  1. Highlight all or some of the text
  2. Select the 'Launch Reader' button
