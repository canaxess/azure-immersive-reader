Immersive Reader

Demo showing how Microsoft Azure cognitive services Immersive Reader can be used to assist a users literacy and comprehension. A website may have sections of text at an advanced reading age, the tool could be used to assist a user to understand individual words, sections of text or the entire content. Additionally it could be used as technique to pass WCAG 2.1 AAA 3.1.5 Reading Level and 3.1.6 Pronunciation Level AAA.

To use:

  1. Highlight text (either mouse or screen select on modile devices )
  2. Click Launch Reader
